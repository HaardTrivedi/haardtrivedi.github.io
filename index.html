Get to Know Me

Hello there (General Kenobi, sorry had to say it) - Welcome to my page!

I am currently working as a Cloud Infrastructure Engineer in the Professional Services Team at Amazon Web Services. My capstone project of creating a Wellness Hub for the students at my university is now avaialble on the Google Play Store.

I was a student at the University of Ottawa where I earned my undergraduate for Bachelor's of Applied Sciences in Software Engineering with magna cum laude honours. I consider myself fluent in 4 languages - English, French, Gujarati and Hindi.

As part of my Mandatory Co-op I have previously worked for Amazon, Micro Focus, CIBC and Global Affairs Canada. I participated in an internship during Summer 2020 at Amazon Web Services in a Cloud Infrastructure Architect role as a Professional Services Intern. During my internships, I have gathered experience in Machine Learning using NumPy and Pandas libraries, cloud infrastructure development with AWS, Big Data while utilizing Hadoop & Cloudera, Data Warehousing, API Development and IT Security.

I am interested in working within the AI/ML, Cloud and Cybersecurity fields. However, I enjoy learning new technologies to enhance my knowledge.

In my spare time, I am a vivid reader, sports enthusiast and a geek of a multitude of TV shows. Among my favourite books are the Harry Potter, LOTR and Percy Jackson series. I cheer for the Toronto Maple Leaafs & Raptors year in year out and love cricket as well (Mumbai Indians/Team India). Also, if there's a sitcom that's created a lot of buzz, I've probably watched it - HIMYM and Brooklyn 99 are my top 2 (please don't ask me to pick 1).
